What does the company/service provide?

The Chattanooga Times Free Press is a daily broadsheet newspaper published in Chattanooga, Tennessee, and is distributed in the metropolitan Chattanooga region of southeastern Tennessee and northwestern Georgia. It is one of Tennessee's major newspapers. Parent Company: WEHCO Media

Who is the target audience for the site?

The primary audience consists of the their readers in the metropolitan Chattanooga region of southeastern Tennessee and northwestern Georgia. The newspaper appeals to both the left and the right and covers a wide variety of topics.

Company's Brand, Personality, and Culture

The Tennessee Press Association recognized the Times Free Press as the best newspaper in Tennessee in 2002. One year later, Editor and Publisher magazine named the Times Free Press as one of 10 newspapers in the United States "doing it right". On Monday, April 14, 2014 the Chattanooga Times Free Press was nominated as a finalist for the 2014 Pulitzer Prize in Local Reporting.

The company's primary goals/objectives?

The Times Free Press main objective is to provide accurate and update news to all of it's subscribers and citizens of Chattanooga and other surrouning areas.

What doesn't works well with the current website?

The current website lacks order or hierachy for the articles displayed. Before the site was updated to what it is now I felt as if articles were on the page with no rhyme or reason. The only thing that grabbed my attenion was the ads, and that is not good. Adding some organization to the clutter and streamlining the content with better hierachy decision will drastically improve this site.
