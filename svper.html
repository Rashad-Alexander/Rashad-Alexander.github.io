What does the company/service provide?

My older brother is a director and he works with a group called Svperlative. They're based in Atlanta and they shoot all over the city. Whether it's a show, music video, or cool event...most likely they're going to be there with the film rolling.

Who is the target audience for the site?

Potential clients who need a good production team.

Company's Brand, Personality, and Culture

Svperlative is all about style. They let their art speak for itself. The subject they choose to film are a great representation of the culture and vibe of the young and connected crowd of Atlanta.

The company's primary goals/objectives?

MAKE DOPE VIDEOS...DUH!

What doesn't works well with the current website?

I told my bro that his site was pretty bland. I wanted to create something that represents everything that Svperlative brings to the table. In an effort to generate more clients he wanted me to implement features that will allow for new people to see what they have to offer, how much will it cost, and how to contact them to get something going.
