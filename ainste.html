What does the company/service provide?

Ainste is a wallet company that makes cool minimalist wallets.

Who is the target audience for the site?

Anybody who needs a cool wallet.

Company's Brand, Personality, and Culture

Ainste, is inspired by Amazon, Einstein, and Steve Jobs. Amazon is a huge retailer that sells millions of cool products. This in turn leads to Einstein who is famous for his ideas and theories. Steve Jobs is a mastermind of breaking the mold and developing creative products. Put these three together and what they stand for, and Ainste was created.

The company's primary goals/objectives?

Ainste makes cool wallets for cool people. Their design isn't just innovative because of it's slim look...but also because of the RFID technology that protects your stuff from identification theft.

What doesn't works well with the current website?

There isn't anything really wrong with the current site. I liked the current design, and I just wanted to put my own spin on it. It's a cool product and I wanted demonstrate my e-Commerce oriented site design skills. Coding it should be fairly easy...I just need to figure out Magneto to get the e-commerce side up and running hahaha.
